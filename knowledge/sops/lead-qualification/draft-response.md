# Draft Response SOP

## Purpose

Draft a follow-up response that matches lead quality and next-step intent.

## Required Inputs

- Lead score from `score-lead`
- `sampleLead`

## Procedure

1. Read the lead score and disposition first.
2. Match the response tone and ask to the disposition.
3. Keep the response short, specific, and easy to send.
4. Avoid overselling if the lead is a nurture case.

## Output Standard

- A concise response draft with clear next-step intent.
- The message should sound like a human operator, not a canned bot.
- The CTA must match the lead quality.

## Failure Conditions

- Message ignores the score or disposition.
- CTA is too aggressive for a nurture lead.
- Draft sounds generic or templated.
