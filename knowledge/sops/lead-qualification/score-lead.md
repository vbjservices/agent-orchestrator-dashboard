# Score Lead SOP

## Purpose

Score inbound leads before sales effort is spent on them.

## Required Inputs

- `sampleLead`
- `qualificationThreshold`

## Procedure

1. Evaluate revenue band, team size, pain level, and source quality.
2. Apply the configured threshold consistently.
3. Recommend a disposition based on the score, not gut feel.
4. Keep the reasoning compact and auditable.

## Output Standard

- Return a numeric score, threshold, and disposition.
- Include the key lead facts used to justify the score.
- Keep the decision easy to review later.

## Failure Conditions

- Score does not map to the disposition.
- Important lead facts are omitted.
- Threshold is ignored or applied inconsistently.
