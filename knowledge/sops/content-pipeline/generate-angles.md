# Generate Angles SOP

## Purpose

Turn research findings into concrete content angles with a clear hook.

## Required Inputs

- Research brief from `research-signals`
- `campaignTheme`
- `callToAction`

## Procedure

1. Select the highest-tension research signals.
2. Convert each signal into a direct angle with a strong opening claim.
3. Keep the angle tied to the audience pain, not just the product.
4. Ensure the CTA can plausibly follow from the angle.

## Output Standard

- Return a short list of distinct angles.
- Each angle needs a title and a one-line hook.
- Angle titles should be specific, not slogan sludge.

## Failure Conditions

- Angles repeat the same claim with cosmetic wording changes.
- Hooks are vague or too broad to script.
- CTA feels bolted on instead of earned.
