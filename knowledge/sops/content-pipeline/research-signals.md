# Research Signals SOP

## Purpose

Identify market signals that are worth turning into content angles.

## Required Inputs

- `campaignTheme`
- `idealCustomerProfile`
- `painPoints`
- `competitors`

## Procedure

1. Extract the strongest buyer pains tied to the campaign theme.
2. Identify recurring objections and language patterns from the target ICP.
3. Compare likely in-house, freelance, and software alternatives.
4. Distill the result into concrete hooks, not generic observations.

## Output Standard

- A focused research brief with a headline and concise bullets.
- Bullets should be specific enough for an ideation step to use immediately.
- Avoid filler claims that cannot influence downstream messaging.

## Failure Conditions

- Findings are generic and could apply to any market.
- Competitor pressure is missing.
- The brief does not surface a usable tension or hook.
